Connect three

Instructions of the game

  • Two players should have to play this game.
  • The players have to click on a grey coloured circle in order to change the colour of that particular circle.
  • If three circles of the same colour goes next to each other in a horizontal line or in a vertical line or along principal diagonals, the player of that particular colour gains one point.
  • The game will not end until all the grey circles are filled with either blue or green.
